Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

What industries do you work with?
I work with real estate, e-commerce, local businesses, D2C brands, education, and service-based companies. If your business needs digital growth, I can help — industry is rarely a barrier.
How long before I see results from SEO?
SEO is a medium-term investment. Most clients see measurable improvements in rankings within 2–4 months, with significant gains by month 5–6. Paid ads can deliver results from day one.
Do you offer monthly retainer packages?
Yes! I offer flexible monthly retainers for ongoing work like social media management, SEO, and ad campaigns, as well as fixed-price one-time projects for websites and audits.
What is your minimum ad budget for Meta Ads?
I recommend a minimum of ₹5,000–₹10,000/month in ad spend to see meaningful results. My management fee is separate and depends on campaign complexity. I always optimize for maximum ROAS.
Who will I be working with — you or the team?
You work directly with me. I am your single point of contact, strategy lead, and project manager. Behind the scenes, I coordinate specialists — but every decision, update, and deliverable comes through me personally.
Do you provide reports and updates?
Yes — every client receives monthly performance reports with clear metrics, insights, and next-step recommendations. I also do regular check-in calls to keep you fully in the loop.
